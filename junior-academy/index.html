ABOUT GARDENDALE JUNIOR ACADEMY

GJA is a comprehensive kindergarten preparatory school which also offers an excellent music training program. Our class sizes are necessarily small to ensure the best attention and education for our students.

The Preschool (K3) and Junior Kindergarten (K4) programs include weekly classical literature selections for discussion and training in listening and comprehension skills, pre-reading and pre-math exercises, daily recitation and memorization practice, and even training in etiquette. For Junior Kindergarten students, a kindergarten-readiness assessment will be administered at the culmination of the program.
